Dal Makhani Recipe

Here are the ingredients and Instructions for making your own Restaurant like Dal Makhani & Rice at home.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup black lentils (urad dal)
  • 1 cup kidney beans (rajma)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the black lentils and kidney beans and soak overnight in 4 cups of water.
  2. In a large saucepan, heat the ghee or oil over medium heat. Add the cumin seeds and wait until they start to crackle.
  3. Add the chopped onion and cook until it becomes soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the minced garlic and ginger and cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they have broken down into a pulp, about 5 minutes.
  6. Add the coriander powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, chili powder, and salt to taste. Cook for another 2 minutes, stirring continuously.
  7. Drain the soaked lentils and kidney beans and add them to the pan. Stir well to combine.
  8. Add 4 cups of water and bring the mixture to a boil. Then reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and let the lentils and beans simmer until they are tender, about 1 hour. Stir occasionally.
  9. When the lentils and beans are cooked, mash some of them with a potato masher or a fork to create a thicker consistency.
  10. Stir in the heavy cream and cook for another 5 minutes.
  11. Serve the dal makhani hot, garnished with fresh cilantro leaves. Enjoy with rice, roti, or naan bread.

Enjoy this delicious and hearty dish from Northern parts of India, perfect for any occasion!
